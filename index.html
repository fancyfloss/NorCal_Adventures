01 — The Modern Wave

The New Guard

🏄

Seafood · Outer Sunset

Hook Fish Co.

4542 Irving St

The ultimate local ritual. Sustainable fish, grilled burritos, and poke. Grab it to-go and eat it on the sand at Ocean Beach as the sun dips.

Pizza · Tenderloin

Filipino Heart

🇵🇭

Fine Dining · Wharf

Abacá

Contemporary Filipino-Californian. Sisig fried rice and ube cheesecake treated with world-class precision.

Fusion · Mission

Señor Sisig

The food truck that became an empire. Filipino-Mexican mashups that defined Bay Area street food.

Hole-in-the-wall · Tenderloin

Kusina Ni Tess

No frills. Tapsilog, adobo, and sinigang just like nanay used to make. Authentic soul for under $15.

02 — The Classics

Counter Institutions

🥃

Raw Bar · Nob Hill

Swan Oyster Depot

18 marble stools. Cash only. Since 1912. Order the crab back and a cold beer. Don't ask for a menu.

Prime Rib · Van Ness

House of Prime Rib

Tableside silver carts and spinning salads. A time capsule of 1940s SF glamour. Book weeks ahead.

Waterfront · Pier 30

Red's Java House

Bourdain's favorite dive. Cheeseburger on sourdough right under the Bay Bridge. Real SF energy.

The Wild Edge

Point Reyes

April is whale migration peak. Look for gray whales from the lighthouse and elk at Tomales Point.

Hawk Hill

The most dramatic Bridge views. April wildflowers are blooming across the Marin Headlands right now.
